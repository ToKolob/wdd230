Welcome to the Cruzeiro do Sul Chamber of Commerce!

Nestled in the heart of the Amazonian countryside, our chamber is dedicated to fostering economic growth and community collaboration in Cruzeiro do Sul. Specializing in fishing, artisanal crafts, and tourism, we provide a platform for businesses to connect, thrive, and contribute to our vibrant local economy.

Join us as we build bridges between entrepreneurs, celebrate our unique cultural heritage, and chart a course towards prosperity in our beloved corner of the Amazon. Welcome to the Cruzeiro do Sul Chamber of Commerce — where opportunities abound, and the spirit of entrepreneurship flourishes.
